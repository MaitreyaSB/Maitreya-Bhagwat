I’m a 19-year-old photographer based in Maharashtra. I picked up my uncle's bridge camera at 14 and eventually found my focus in black-and-white street and documentary work.

This site is my experimental space for unscripted street shots. On the commercial side, I run Metal Eye Visual Studios for industrial and product photography, and in my downtime, I shoot wildlife.

I still have a lot to learn. This is just the start.
